How Saiyan Levels Work

Earn 100 points to upgrade your Saiyan level.

  • Kaioken (0-1000)
  • Super Saiyan (1000-5000)
  • Super Saiyan 2 (5000-15000)
  • Super Saiyan 3 (15000-40000)
  • Super Saiyan God (40000-120000)
  • Super Saiyan Blue (120000-250000)
  • Ultra Instinct (250000-400000)
  • Mastered Ultra Instinct (400000-800000)

How Points Work

This is how this app works and how points work.

You will add tasks. Depending on the difficulty you'll add points to it.
Highest difficulty point is 5.
And if you don't complete a task you've a penalty button. You have to penalize yourself manually.

Penalty point depends on the difficulty of that task.
This is the system:
For 5 difficulty task, penalty is 7
For 4 difficulty task, penalty is 6
For 3 difficulty task, penalty is 4
For 2 difficulty task, penalty is 2
For 1 difficulty task, penalty is 1

But if you reach Saiyan points 15000 means you're now super Saiyan then you can add tasks up to difficulty level 10 and from that point, there won't be any penalty points.
Reason for no penalty?
You've already achieved so much that you know what you're doing. If you don't complete a task by that time, you probably have something better to do. No penalty.

And after reaching 120000 points you can choose task difficulty up to 30. Because at that time your task will be of more importance for sure. You might be a millionaire by that time.

Hold yourself accountable and every morning hit the penalty button if you don't complete any task.
And there's a task reset button. day It'll reset your task. But the points will be the same. You have to do it manually every day.

And there's a reset all option. It'll reset all your progress

Remember to export your data continuously after a few weeks. To keep your data safe from unnecessary data loss.
